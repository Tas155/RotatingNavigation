All About Cats

By Tas

If you ask me whether I'm a dog or cat lover, I guess judging by the title of this article, you can already tell, I am a huge cat lover. I have wanted a kitten for as long as I can remember. However, growing up in a small apartment, along with parents who love their leather furniture, to this day I have yet to adopt a kitten as my own. However, my dream to adopt a cat still stands, I think cats make great companions and cuddle buddies as well, especially for people who live alone.
